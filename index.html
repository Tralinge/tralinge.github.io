Tralinge - A Site Dedicated to Langauge Acquisition

This site pulls together the knowledge of over a decade of combined teaching experience at multiple levels of intensity (casual to university level) and several age groups from infant to adult. This site is still under development, so please be patient while we learn to code and add more material. In the mean time, feel free to browse the different pages here. Currently the site is divided into Chinese and English, with more languages in the future.

For my English learners, please note the "Worksheets" section where you can find several useful exercises.

My current project is to make short phonics books to help English learners practice differentiating between diagraphs (e.g. ch/sh, sk/sc, pr/pl) in an amusing way. Be sure to check out these short stories here.

Tralinge 川林哥

有一天我要走遍天涯，学遍各种语言。
One day, I will put my foot on every continent and learn as many languages as I can.

川林哥是我们目前一起创作的网站，分享学习语言的经历。
Tralinge is a new project that we are working on. Our goal is to build a website where we can share our language learning strategies, create our own learning content, and provide a place to look for resources.

欢迎有空来逛逛，给我们提提意见！
也可以告诉我们你希望能够学到什么，搞不好会有惊喜哦！
Welcome to our website. It is still being built, but feel free to explore - you never know where it might take you.

你的梦想是什么呢？你的行动又在哪里呢？
That is my dream. What is yours? What are you doing to get you closer to your dream?

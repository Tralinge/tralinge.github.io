Christopher - 林风

Hello, I'm Christopher!
I'm a teacher from the United States with over 6 years of experience teaching various subjects such as English, Spanish, science, and math. Prior to teaching, I worked in microbiology and chemistry labs, but found my true love is teaching - whether you are 3 years old or 100, I'm sure we can learn from each other. I have a wide variety of interests, but above all, love traveling and learning languages, which allows me to engage my students and help them by focusing on their language-specific mistakes.

Pretérito

The preterite tense is used to denote events that have been completed in the past. For comparison to English, "I jumped" is preterite, whereas, "I was jumping" is the past imperfect.

Regular Preterite

Subject -ar Verbs-er and -ir Verbs

él, ella, usted-ó-ió

The Lion Sleeps Tonight

Simba had lost his father and left his pride (group of lions). Now living with other animals in the jungle, he is resting far away from the dangers presented by his uncle. This song expresses Simba’s feeling of calm after the ordeals. The phrase “Aweemoweh” should actually be “Uyimbube", which is Zulu (a language of Southern Africa) for “You are a lion”.

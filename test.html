Colors of the Wind

Pocahontas was a Native American, who lived near the English settlement of Jamestown, Virginia (USA). Born around the 15th century (~1600), she was the daughter of a powerful native leader. During this time, there was a lot of anger between the native groups and the English settlers, partly based on cultural differences. These different views of the world are what Pocahontas is singing about in this song.

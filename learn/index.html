How to Learn

As an ESL teacher for the last several years, I have met a diverse group of students, from South America to the Middle East and Asia. For me, languages have always been a bit of a passion, which is why I have learned to converse in various languages and at least get my point across even with extremely limited knowledge of a language. After my students learn that I can speak a few different languages they never cease to ask the question “what is the secret to learning languages?” I have created this section to discuss my thoughts and bring you my top tips.

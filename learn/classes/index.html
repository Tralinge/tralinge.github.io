Individual Classes

Hello, my name is Christopher and I have been teaching for over five years. In this time I have taught students from many different countries such as China, Brazil, Saudi Arabia, Korea, Bolivia, etc. From 4 years old to 100 years old, I can adapt my teaching style to match your level and needs to give you a unique experience, whether you are interested in learning English, science, or just need some help preparing for an exam or an interview.

I speak a few languages already such as Spanish, Mandarin Chinese, and French as well as very basic speech in several languages such as Russian, Arabic, Thai, Portuguese, and Turkish. I have found that this knowledge helps me better understand where my students are having difficulties when learning English and how I can better help them. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me here or book a lesson by clicking on the icons that say “book now” and I will get in touch with you.

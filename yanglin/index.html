关于我 about me:

My name means: 阳 yáng means to the sun, sunshine, or as in yinyang - masculine, strong. It also refers to the place I was born, guǎngyáng county. 琳 lín means beautiful jade which Chinese adore. If you separate the character, it becomes 王, king and 林, woods since I was born in the woods. Put everything together and you have the story. :)

Currently a graduate student and a team teacher dedicated to language education. Striving to empower students and make the world a better place!

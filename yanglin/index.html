你好啊！Hello!

I am Yanglin, from rural China, professionally trained in both China and the US.

I have been working as a language teacher for about a decade. Language empowers

me and I want to pass the power on to more people who are willing to learn a

different culture and to see the world from a different perspective. Learning

a foreign language gives you a new way of living. I am here to help.

Coming from China, my name carries the good wishes from my family.

阳 yáng means to the sun, sunshine, or as in yinyang -

masculine, strong. It also refers to the place I was born, guǎngyáng

county. 琳 lín means beautiful jade which Chinese adore. If you

separate the character, it becomes 王, king and 林, woods since I was

born in the woods. Put everything together and you have the story. :)

I am looking forward to create more stories with you.

Curriculum Design- Culture-Based Chinese Course

Jan 2019 to Present
How to teach Chinese through cultural projects assisted by technology, such as Wechat. WeChat is a very popular app used by Chinese speaking community.

It is a personalized curriculum design based on empirical research and my own teaching experience. I intend to connect my students to native speakers of Chinese and though the projects they can improve not only their language skills but also their cultural competencies and prepare them to be global citizens.

Translaguaging Stories from Multilingual Speakers

Jan 2019 to Present
If you happen to speak more than one language, you must have the experience of using words from more than just one languages together to articulate your thoughts or you might use the language structure from one language to create new utterances in another language.

It is so fascinating to see and record the beauty of languages. As a multilingual myself, I often code-switching from my dialect, Mandarin, Spanish to English. In school, our teachers were not speaking Mandarin as they were required to but instead using our shared language, the local dialect. I use Mandarin when I communicate to people not from the little area I am from. And now I am in the US, English is often the language I use to speak to my professors and students. As a Chinese teacher, I often mash Chinese and English together. When I speak to my friends who speak more than one languages, we would mix languages all the time, be it English and Mandarin, English, Mandarin and Spanish. It has become part of me to draw different language resources.
